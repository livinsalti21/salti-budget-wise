# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: January 2025  
**Last Updated**: January 2025

## Introduction

Livin Salti ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the Service.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address, username, and profile information
- **Financial Data**: Budget categories, saving goals, and transaction amounts (amounts only, not account details)
- **Profile Data**: Display name, avatar image, and preferences you choose to share
- **Communications**: Messages you send us for support or feedback

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the app, features used, and time spent
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Analytics Data**: App performance, crash reports, and usage patterns
- **Push Notification Tokens**: To send you notifications (with your permission)

### Information from Third Parties
- **Social Features**: If you choose to connect with friends, we may access public profile information with your consent
- **Analytics Providers**: We use services like PostHog for analytics (when enabled)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

### Core App Functionality
- Provide budgeting and saving tools
- Calculate compound interest projections
- Track your financial goals and achievements
- Sync your data across devices

### Social Features
- Enable sharing achievements with friends (only what you choose to share)
- Facilitate group challenges and social motivation
- Display leaderboards and community features

### Communication
- Send push notifications for saving reminders (with your permission)
- Provide customer support
- Send important service updates

### Improvement and Analytics
- Analyze app usage to improve features
- Fix bugs and optimize performance
- Develop new features based on user behavior

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

### With Your Consent
- Social features you explicitly enable
- Information you choose to make public

### Service Providers
- Cloud hosting and data storage (Supabase)
- Analytics providers (PostHog, if enabled)
- Push notification services (Firebase)
- Customer support tools

### Legal Requirements
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect our rights and safety
- To investigate fraud or security issues
- In connection with business transfers

## Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Access Controls**: Strict limits on who can access your data
- **Regular Audits**: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
- **Secure Infrastructure**: Enterprise-grade cloud hosting with Supabase

However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Your Privacy Rights and Choices

### Account Control
- **Access**: View your personal information in app settings
- **Update**: Modify your profile and preferences
- **Delete**: Request account deletion (contact support)

### Communication Preferences
- **Push Notifications**: Disable in app settings or device settings
- **Marketing**: Opt out of promotional communications

### Social Features
- **Privacy Settings**: Control what information you share
- **Friend Connections**: Manage your social connections
- **Public Profiles**: Choose what appears in your public profile

### Data Portability
- **Export**: Request a copy of your data
- **Transfer**: Move your data to another service (where technically feasible)

## Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the Service and for legitimate business purposes:

- **Account Data**: Until you delete your account
- **Usage Analytics**: Up to 2 years for improvement purposes
- **Financial Data**: Until account deletion (for goal tracking continuity)
- **Support Communications**: Up to 3 years for quality assurance

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where required by law to retain it longer.

## California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

- **Right to Know**: What personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell
- **Right to Delete**: Request deletion of your personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out**: Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell)
- **Right to Non-Discrimination**: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@livinsalti.com.
